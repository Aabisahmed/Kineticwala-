Meet the Founder

About Aabis Ahmed

Kinetic Wala is founded by Aabis Ahmed — an educator passionate about building strong academic foundations in students from a young age.

The goal is to simplify complex academic topics and help students develop strong conceptual clarity in Science and Mathematics, making even the most difficult problems feel approachable and solvable.

The platform also introduces students to robotics and practical innovation, helping them connect academic knowledge with real-world engineering thinking and building the problem-solvers of tomorrow.

🎯 Mission: Make quality education accessible and impactful for every student in Class 7–10.
🔬 Focus: Deep conceptual clarity in Science and Mathematics through structured, logical learning.
⚙️ Vision: Bridge academics and innovation through robotics and hands-on engineering learning.
